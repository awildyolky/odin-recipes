Baked Potato Recipe

Description

Extremely simple dish to make. All you need is a russet potato, access to an oven, and whatever topping you may desire!

Ingredients

  • Russet Potato
  • Toppings: I recommend butter, sour cream, and chives.
  • Seasoning: salt and pepper. Rosemary can also be a nice touch.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Rinse off potato and use a fork to poke holes along its spine. This prevents potato explosion.
  3. Place potato on oven rack and cook for 45 min to 1 hour, until potato has softened.
  4. Remove potato from oven and cut lengthwise, aka, potato spine. Add desired toppings, including seasoning, and viola, potato is ready for consumption!
