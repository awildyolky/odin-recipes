Easy Ramen Recipe

Description

An extremely straightforward recipe to spice up a simple pack of ramen into something delicious.

Ingredients

  • Pack of Ramen
  • Oil or butter
  • Garlic
  • Soysauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Red chili pepper flakes
  • Seasame oil
  • Peeled hard boiled egg
  • Green onion
  • Optional: protein, such as tofu or sliced pork, if you want an even heartier helping.

Steps

  1. Prepare noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat up oil and throw in the garlic.
  3. After garlic has started to brown, mix in a teaspoon of brown sugar, a couple tablespoons of soysauce, and the chili pepper flakes.
  4. Mix in the cooked ramen noodles until evenly coated with sauce.
  5. Add the hardboiled egg, green onion, and drizzle noodles with a teaspoon of seasame oil.
  6. Your ramen is ready! Enjoy.
