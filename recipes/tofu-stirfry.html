Tofu Stirfry

Description

A delicious and simple recipe for tofu stirfry. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tofu
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Cabbage
  • Green onion
  • Veggie oil
  • Soysauce
  • Garlic (or garlic powder)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Red chili pepper flakes
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker, two parts water to one part rice. (Ex: 2 cups water for 1 cup of rice).
  2. Cut tofu into cubes, and saute in a pan with a splash of veggie oil until lightly browned.
  3. Remove tofu from pan and set to the side.
  4. Chop all veggies into desired size.
  5. Add more oil to the pan, and saute all of the vegetables except for the green onion, which will act as a garnish.
  6. Mix ingredients for the sauce: soysauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, garlic powder, and pepper flakes. Only a teaspoon of sugar needed.
  7. Once veggies have started to soften, return the tofu to the pan, along with the sauce.
  8. Cook until the sauce is reduced, stirring well so veggies and tofu are all coated.
  9. Depending on preference, can either mix the rice in with the sauted tofu and veggies, or simply add veggie/tofu stirfry onto a bowl of rice.
  10. Done. You should now have one heaping bowl of tasty tofu stirfry :)
